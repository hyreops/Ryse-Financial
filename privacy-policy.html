Privacy Policy

RYSE Financial

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

At Meyers Enterprise LLC, doing business as RYSE Financial (“we,” “us,” or “our”), your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (https://rysefinancial.us) or engage with us through any of our online or offline services. By using our website and services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Voluntarily provided data — such as when you fill out forms, submit an application, or contact us
  • Automatically collected data — such as browser type, IP address, and pages visited

Personal Information

We may request personal details including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Employment or background information you choose to provide
  • Social media profiles

This information is only collected when voluntarily submitted by you, for example when:

  • Submitting an application or inquiry through our website
  • Signing up for updates
  • Contacting us through email, phone, or social media

Log & Usage Data

Our servers may automatically collect log data, which may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Date/time of your visit
  • Error diagnostics and technical data

While this data does not directly identify you, it may be linked with other information to help enhance your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Review and process your application or inquiry
  • Communicate with you about the opportunity and respond to questions
  • Schedule calls and follow up with applicants
  • Conduct analytics and improve our website and services
  • Send updates or promotions where you have opted in
  • Fulfill legal, regulatory, and administrative requirements

We will never use your information for purposes that are incompatible with these uses.

SMS Consent and Mobile Messaging

If you provide your phone number and opt in to receive SMS messages from RYSE Financial, we may use your phone number to send text messages related to application updates, interview and call scheduling, opportunity information, and customer support. If you separately opt in to marketing messages, we may also send promotional texts, updates, and information about RYSE Financial.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You may reply STOP to opt out at any time or HELP for assistance.

SMS opt-in consent and phone numbers collected for SMS purposes will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

For more information about our SMS terms, please review our Terms of Service at https://rysefinancial.us/terms-of-service/.

Legal Basis for Processing

We only collect personal data where we have a legitimate reason, such as:

  • You have given consent
  • It is necessary to respond to your inquiry or application
  • We are legally obligated to do so
  • We have a legitimate interest, such as improving user experience or customer service

Security of Your Information

We protect your personal data using commercially reasonable safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or loss.

However, no digital system is completely secure. While we do our best, we cannot guarantee absolute data security. You are responsible for securing your passwords and account access credentials.

Data Breach Notification Compliance: In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with the applicable laws of the State of Missouri and any other applicable federal or state laws.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or comply with legal, accounting, or reporting obligations. Once it is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for adults 18 and older and are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Internal staff, contractors, and business affiliates
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, communication tools, etc.)
  • Legal or regulatory bodies when required

We do not sell your data to third parties. As stated above, SMS opt-in consent and phone numbers collected for SMS purposes are never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Withdraw consent to marketing or communication
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise your rights, please contact us at the email listed below.
